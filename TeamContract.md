# Team Contract

## Purpose of this Contract
This contract establishes the shared expectations and commitments for how our team will collaborate during the course. Its purpose is to promote accountability, professionalism, and mutual support as we complete individual preparation, readiness assurance tests, in-class activities, and our course project.

---

## Team Norms and Expectations

### Communication
- **Platform**: All team communication outside of class will occur via Instagram.
- **Response Time**: Team members will respond to messages within one business day on weekdays and within two days on weekends.
- **Notification Obligations**: Team members will notify the group if they:
    - Are unable to meet a deadline.
    - Encounter challenges or bugs they cannot resolve independently.
    - Will miss a lecture or scheduled team meeting.
    - Propose changes to the project structure.
    - Want to select specific tasks for themselves.
- **Respectful and Inclusive Behavior**: Team members will:
    - Fairly evaluate and consider everyone’s ideas.
    - Ensure all members have the opportunity to contribute meaningfully to the project.
    - Distribute tasks equitably among team members.

---

### Additional Team Norms and Expectations
- Each team member will actively participate in discussions and support others when needed.
- Constructive feedback will be delivered respectfully.
- Deadlines and commitments will be met to maintain team progress.

---

## Decision Making
- Team decisions will be made through discussion and by actively listening to each member’s ideas.
- For situations where consensus cannot be reached, decisions will be finalized by a majority vote.

---

## Conflict Resolution
- Conflicts will be resolved by listening to each team member’s perspective and working toward a compromise.
- If a resolution cannot be reached internally, the team may consult a TA or instructor for guidance.

---

## Accountability
- Each team member is responsible for completing their assigned tasks in a timely manner.
- Team members will seek assistance from teammates, TAs, or instructors when required.
- Each member will contribute fairly to all aspects of the project and support the team in achieving shared goals.

---

By signing below, we acknowledge that we have read, discussed, and agreed to these terms. We understand that this contract is binding for the duration of the course and may only be revised with the consent of all team members.
