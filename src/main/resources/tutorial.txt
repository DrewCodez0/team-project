HOW TO PLAY WORDLE
====================

OBJECTIVE:
- Guess the 5 letter long target word in 6 attempts or fewer.

- The target word could have more than one of the same letter.

- You will have 6 attempts to guess the target word. Good luck!

TIPS:
- Start with words that have common vowels (A, E, I, O, U)
- Pay attention to which letters are correct but in wrong positions
- Use the process of elimination

STATISTICS AND SAVING YOUR SCORES:
- Your game statistics are tracked automatically.
- You can view / import / export your statistics in the "Stats" page.
- You can export your score using the "Export" button in the result screen.
